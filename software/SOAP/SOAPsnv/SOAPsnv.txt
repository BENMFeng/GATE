Assume 'T/chr1.soap.sorted.gz' is SOAP result(sorted, merged and duplication removed) for Tumor sample, 
       'N/chr1.soap.sorted.gz' is SOAP result(sorted, merged and duplication removed) for Normal sample.


Follow the steps:

1. Use 'SOAPpileup' to observe allele frequency of base A, T, C and G at each locus.

  Example:
		../SOAPpileup -r reference/hg19.fa.gz -l reference/hg19.region -s T/chr1.soap.sorted.gz -o T/chr1.pileup.gz -d
		../SOAPpileup -r reference/hg19.fa.gz -l reference/hg19.region -s N/chr1.soap.sorted.gz -o N/chr1.pileup.gz -d


2. 'SNVtest' uses Fisher's Exact Test to detect sites with significantly allele frequency change between Normal and Tumor tissues.

  Example:
		../SNVtest N/chr1.pileup.gz T/chr1.pileup.gz -o chr1.test.gz


3. 'alleleQC' ascertains allele quality for each sites using Pileup result.

  Example:
		../alleleQC T/chr1.pileup.gz T/chr1.pileup.qc.gz 
		../alleleQC N/chr1.pileup.gz N/chr1.pileup.qc.gz 


4. 'SNVdetect' detects SNV from N/T cancer pairs, 'chr1.SNV.gz' is the final SNV result.

  Example:
		../SNVdetect reference/hg19.chr.length.txt N/chr1.pileup.qc.gz T/chr1.pileup.qc.gz chr1.test.gz chr1.SNV.gz
